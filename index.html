HACKBLAGOEVGRAD 2016

About HackBlagoevgrad

HackBlagoevgrad is a week-long IT forum which will be held for the first time in Blagoevgrad. It combines the formats of an IT conference and a hackathon.

The conference will take part between the 14th and the 18th of November and will consist of several presentations and workshops given by professionals in the field.

The competitive part – the hackathon will be a three day event between the 18th and the 20th of November during which participants will work in previously formed teams. The topic for the hackathon is Hack to the Next Level. This means that the participants will be encouraged to create innovative products that can solve problems on a local and national scale.

The Prizes for the hackathon are as follows:

  • 1st Place: 2500 leva
  • 2nd Place: 1500 leva
  • 3rd Place: 750 leva

Schedule

As scheduled, the first HackBlagoevgrad event will go in the following way:

Monday through Thursday (November 14-17, 2016): Starting at 6:00PM companies’ professionals give presentations/workshops in one of the aulas in Balkanski Academic Center, AUBG Campus.

Friday (November 18, 2016): The last round of lectures starts at 4PM and around 7PM participants and mentors gather for the kick-off of the hackathon weekend. The participants discuss their ideas and form teams (if they have not done so previously at the time of registration or during the presentations’/workshops’ days). After that, the teams head for their personal workspaces in the Sports Hall of the ABF Student Center, AUBG Campus, and start working.

Saturday (November 19, 2016): The participants continue working on their ideas throughout the whole day/night and the mentors help them.

Sunday (November 20, 2016): The participants finish up their projects. In the evening participants, mentors, and spectators gather in Dr. Carl Djerassi Theater Hall, AUBG Campus. Every team presents what they have done in front of the jury and an open audience. After that comes the voting and the jury announces the winners who receive prizes.

Organizers

We, the people behind the scenes of HackBlagoevgrad, are a group of young enthusiasts who believe in the future of technology and in the abilities of our fellows to take the initiative and make a change.

Currently, the main responsibility for the organization of the week-long event is shared between The Hub at the American University in Bulgaria, the High School of Mathematics “Acad. Sergey Pavlovich Korolyov” in Blagoevgrad, and other devotees to our common mission. Clearly, our dreams would not have become a reality without the special help and support granted by our partners from HackBulgaria.
