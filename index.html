HACKBLAGOEVGRAD 2016

About HackBlagoevgrad

HackBlagoevgrad is a week-long IT forum which will be held for the first time in Blagoevgrad. It combines the formats of an IT conference and a hackathon.

The conference will take part between the 14th and the 18th of November and will consist of several presentations and workshops given by professionals in the field.

The competitive part – the hackathon will be a three day event between the 18th and the 20th of November during which participants will work in previously formed teams. The topic for the hackathon is Hack to the Next Level. This means that the participants will be encouraged to create innovative products that can solve problems on a local and national scale.

The Prizes for the hackathon are as follows:

  • 1st Place: 2500 leva
  • 2nd Place: 1500 leva
  • 3rd Place: 750 leva

FAQ

Who can attend?

Everyone is welcomed! Students, professional developers, enthusiasts, hobbyist, hackers, designers, entrepreneurs, etc.

Can I attend just the hackathon?

Yes, feel free to do that. We highly encourage you to take part in the conference as well, though, since the lectures will be of great use.

What should I bring?

Everyone should bring with him/her a laptop for the hackathon. If you want to do a hardware hack, don't forget to bring the devices you plan on working with.

How much is this going to cost me?

Admission is free for all! Food and drinks are on us. Seats are limited though so be quick to register!

What can my team build?

Whatever hardware, software, or technology-related projects you and your team can come up with! Projects should not be developed in advance and they will be judged based on creativity, technical difficulty, polish, and usefulness.

Should the projects be uploaded to GitHub?

Yes, and they should be public from the very beginning. We will keep the list of the repositiries in order to monitor for cheating. We will share the list with the jury, but not with the rest of the participants.

Teams?

Teams may contain up to 5 people and a minimum of 2. If you don't have a team prior to HackBlagoevgrad, that's completely fine - we will be holding a team-matching session at the beginning of the hacking period.

What if I have a question that wasn't answered here?

We’re here for you! Send us an email at info@hackblagoevgrad.org or feel free to reach out to us on Facebook or Twitter.
